# Superstore Sales and Profit Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This Tableau dashboard provides insights into sales and profit performance across regions, customer segments, and product sub-categories.

## Key Insights

### 1. Sales and Profit Trend Analysis
- Sales generally increased from 2019 to 2022.
- Highest monthly sales reached approximately 118K.
- Some months showed high sales but relatively lower profit.

### 2. Region and Segment Analysis
- West region generated the highest sales.
- East region was the second-best performing region.
- Consumer segment contributed the largest share of sales.

### 3. Region and Sub-Category Analysis
- Copiers, Phones, and Accessories generated high profits.
- Tables and Bookcases showed losses in several regions.
- West and East regions performed better across most sub-categories.

### 4. Region-wise Sales Distribution
- East: 34.23%
- West: 33.26%
- Central: 19.02%
- South: 13.50%
